Who am I?

My name is Dallin Curtis! I've been a MAJOR coaster nerd my whole life! To me, nothing beats the pure adrenaline rush I feel when I'm strapped on a roller coaster, flying through all kinds of manuevers and feeling the intensity as I travel through these beautiful creations! I also can't help but admire a well-themed park! It started when I took my first ride on "Rock-N-Roller Coaster" at Disney World. Since then, I've been hooked on roller coasters. I've visted many theme parks and have ridden TONS of coasters!

Why did I create Coaster Quest?

I created this page because I want to share my love of roller coasters and theme parks! I've learned a lot over my years of being an enthusiast, and I want to share it with those who want to learn more about these beautiful creations! I want to share:

  • How roller coasters became to be.
  • Coaster-Lingo.
  • The best parks to go to.
  • Tips I've used to enhance my park experience!

My hope is that I can make other people just as enthusiastic and passionate about roller coasters as I am!
